How It Works

  1. Contact me via Telegram, Signal, or Session. Tell me the amount and direction (buy or sell XMR).
  2. We agree on terms — amount, price, payment method, and whether to use escrow.
  3. Execute the trade — for escrow trades, funds lock in Haveno 2-of-3 multisig. For direct trades, we exchange simultaneously.
  4. Done — both parties confirm, escrow releases. Fast, private, no KYC.

With 683 completed trades and 100% feedback across LocalMonero and AgoraDesk, I bring years of proven experience to a market that needs trustworthy counterparties more than ever. Escrow via Haveno multisig means you never have to trust me with your funds — the protocol enforces fair settlement.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does Cash by Mail work?

You send EUR cash in a registered/tracked letter to a designated address. Once received, XMR is released to your wallet (or vice versa for selling). Registered mail provides tracking and insurance. Typical delivery within the EU takes 2-5 business days.

How is the trade secured?

Escrow is available via Haveno (RetoSwap or DawnSwap), which uses 2-of-3 multisig. Both parties and a neutral arbitrator each hold one key. Funds can only be released when 2 of 3 parties agree. This means neither party can steal funds unilaterally.

More Questions

What if my cash gets lost in the mail?

Always use registered/tracked mail. This provides proof of delivery, a tracking number, and insurance coverage. In the rare event of loss, the postal insurance covers the value. We only process trades against confirmed registered mail deliveries.

Can I buy Monero without an exchange account?

Yes — that's the whole point of P2P trading. You need only a Monero wallet (Feather Wallet, Cake Wallet, or the official GUI wallet) and cash. No exchange account, no bank verification, no identity documents. Download a wallet, generate an address, and contact me to start a trade.

How long does a typical trade take?

Face-to-face: minutes. Cash by mail within Germany: 1-2 business days for delivery, XMR released same day upon receipt. Cross-border EU mail: 2-5 business days. Once cash is confirmed, XMR is sent immediately — Monero transactions confirm in under 2 minutes.

Is P2P Monero trading legal in Germany and the EU?

Peer-to-peer trading of cryptocurrency for cash between private individuals is legal in Germany and across the EU. No commercial license is required for occasional private transactions. The MiCA regulation applies to centralized exchanges and custodial services, not to private peer-to-peer trades.

Where did LocalMonero and AgoraDesk go?

LocalMonero shut down in May 2024 and AgoraDesk followed in November 2024. Both platforms ceased operations voluntarily. The P2P Monero community has since migrated to decentralized alternatives: Haveno (RetoSwap, DawnSwap), XMRBazaar, OpenMonero, and direct P2P trading via Telegram, Signal, and Matrix.

What is Haveno escrow and how does it protect me?

Haveno uses 2-of-3 multisig escrow. When a trade begins, both the buyer and seller deposit funds into a multisig wallet. Three keys exist: yours, mine, and a neutral arbitrator's. Releasing funds requires 2 of 3 keys, so neither party can steal. If a dispute arises, the arbitrator reviews evidence and sides with the honest party. This is the gold standard for trustless P2P trading.
